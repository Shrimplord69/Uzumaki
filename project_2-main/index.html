Team Name

After many discussions, we came up with a team name in the Japanese language. “Uzumaki” means a whirlpool containing different elements inside it but always moving together. The initial concept of this name is for our prototype as it is a marketplace that gathers several sellers on a platform but all share the same targeted segmentation. However, the name also has a special meaning representing our team nature. Even though we have members from different majors, different educational backgrounds and ages, we are all willing to work together and support each other to come up with the possible ideas and best outcome. By doing that, we understand each member's weaknesses and strengths to contribute the right value thriving on advantages as a team.

Group Process

Our group did amazing work since everyone committed to finishing quality work and keeping track of each phrase and new changes. Minh was in charge of web development, Thang supported Minh in researching information to enhance the prototype. Bao was in charge of collecting ideas and analysing the markets. Ha broke down the project into stages for easy following tasks with the support from Vu. In this Assignment, we should have improved on our time management, but we do not have the intention to change any working roles because everyone was ready to contribute their best in any allocated tasks.
